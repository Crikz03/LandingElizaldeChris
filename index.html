Favorite Series and Movies

Back to the Future Trilogy

This is one of my favorite movie trilogies, from the charisma of the actors to the main idea of time travel for the era in which it was released. The special effects simply blew my mind.

Stranger Things

This is one of my current favorite series. It came out in 2016 and I started watching it in 2017. I thought it was really good, offering a great variety of characters along with their development through the seasons. Season 4 is my favorite so far and I’m waiting for season 5 :D.

Spider Man 2 (2002)

This is my favorite Spider-Man movie. I watched it when I was only 5 years old and I loved it. It has very memorable moments like the fight between Spider-Man and Doctor Octopus, the redemption of the latter, and the revelation of Spider-Man’s identity to Harry Osborn.

Avengers: Infinity War

This movie has a very special place in my heart because I grew up watching MCU movies, from Iron Man 1 up to this movie, which concluded the Infinity Saga. After 10 years of build-up we had the union of all the heroes in a single film, resulting in one of the best superhero movies to date and a cinema event that was only repeated in its sequel (ENDGAME).

Logan

This movie is one of my favorites within the X-Men universe, as it’s the perfect farewell for such a beloved character. Hugh Jackman gave his last performance as Wolverine in Fox’s universe, gifting us emotional moments that made more than one shed a tear.
